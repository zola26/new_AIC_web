Main Service


The main products and services offered by the AI Center revolve around supporting and empowering individuals interested in AI.

The center provides a comprehensive range of services, including the creation of an interdisciplinary research environment, the provision of state-of-the-art resources, mentorship programs, and the organization of knowledge-sharing platforms.

The focus is on promoting ethical and responsible AI applications. These offerings aim to enable individuals to excel in AI research, develop innovative solutions, and address real-world problems using AI technology.
